Variables¶

  • \(A\) - Wave amplitude (m), half of the wave height (m)
  • \(A(\omega)\) - Frequency dependent radiation added mass
  • \(B(\omega)\) - Frequency dependent radiation wave damping
  • \(\beta\) - Wave direction (Degrees)
  • \(\eta\) - Incident wave (m)
  • \(f\) - Wave frequency (Hz)
  • \(F_{B}(t)\) - Net buoyancy restoring force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(F_{exc}({\omega})\) - Wave excitation force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(F_{rad}(t)\) - Wave radiation force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(F_{PTO}(t)\) - Power take-off force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(F_{ME}(t)\) - Morison element force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(F_{v}(t)\) - Damping or friction force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(F_{m}(t)\) - Mooring connection force (N) or torque (N.m)
  • \(g\) - Gravity
  • \(H\) - Wave height (m)
  • \(H_{s}\) - Significant wave height, mean wave height of the tallest third of waves (m)
  • \(H_{m0}\) - Spectrally derived significant wave height (m)
  • \(k\) - Wave number
  • \(K_{hs}\) - linear hydrostatic restoring coefficient
  • \(m\) - Mass of body (kg)
  • \(m0\) - Spectral moment
  • \(\omega\) - Wave frequency (rad/s)
  • \(\phi\) - Wave phase (rad)
  • \(R_{f}\) - Ramp function
  • \(\rho\) - Water density (kg/m3)
  • \(T_{p}\) - Peak period (s)
  • \(T_{e}\) - Energy period* (s)
  • \(t_{r}\) - Ramp time (s)
  • \(t\) - Simulation time (s)
